How to Use

1. Getting Started

  • 🔧 Setup: Input your current in-game tech levels.
  • 📅 Plan: Queue future tech upgrades. Clicking nodes here adds them to your future timeline.
  • 📜 Logs: View the planned schedule. Click any row in the log to edit it.

2. Basic Controls

  • Left Click: Level Up (+1).
  • Right Click: Level Down (-1) or Remove from Plan.
  • Shift + Click: Max out the node (Setup mode only).
