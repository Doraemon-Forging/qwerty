How to Use

1. Getting Started

  • 🔧 Setup: Input your current in-game tech levels.
  • 📅 Plan: Queue future tech upgrades. Clicking nodes here adds them to your future timeline.
  • 📜 Logs: View the planned schedule. Click any row in the log to edit it. (Mobile only)
  • Calc: Open the Forge Calculator to calculate hammer efficiency, gold gain, and forge upgrades.
  • Egg: Open the Egg Planner to manage your hatching schedule.

2. Managing Your Plan (Logs)

Click or tap any item in the Log List to reveal the Action Menu:

  • 🗑️ Delete: Removes this specific step from the plan. Automatic repair will try to fix the queue if removing it breaks dependencies.
  • ✅ Done: Marks the upgrade as complete. This permanently adds it to your "Setup," removes it from the queue, and syncs the specific time to the "Start Date & Time."
  • ➕ Delay: Adds a waiting period (in minutes) after this step. Use this to account for delays due to sleep, work, or other reasons.
  • ⤵️ Insert: Activates Insert Mode. The next node you click in the Tree will be squeezed in immediately after this step, instead of at the very end of the queue.

3. Basic Controls

  • Left Click / Tap: Level Up (+1).
  • Right Click / Long Press: Level Down (-1) or Remove from Plan.
  • Shift + Click:Max out the node instantly. (Setup mode + Desktop only)

4. Tools

  • 💾 Save / 📂Load: Backup your data to a JSON file.
  • 📊 Summary: View total stats from each tech nodes before and after your planned tech upgrades.
  • 📱 Forge Calculator: Calculate hammer efficiency, gold gain, and forge upgrades.
  • 🥚 Egg Planner: Manage your egg hatching schedule. It automatically accounts for planned tech upgrades to give you accurate hatch times.
